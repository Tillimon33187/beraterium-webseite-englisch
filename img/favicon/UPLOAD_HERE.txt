Browser tab icon (favicon) — separate from header logos in img/logo-en/

Upload ONE file here:
  fav.png         — preferred filename (your browser tab icon)
  favicon.png     — alternative filename

Min. 192×192 px recommended. Transparent background OK.

Then regenerate:
  cd Webseite/site-en
  .venv/bin/python _gen_favicon.py
  .venv/bin/python _gen_pages.py

This creates favicon.ico, icon.png and apple-touch-icon.png in the site root.
